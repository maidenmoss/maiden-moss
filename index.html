About

The maker

Maiden Moss is a one-person studio based in Georgetown, Texas. Everything I make is by hand and one of a kind, born from my own creative inspiration and skills.

I'm drawn to work that feels rooted in natural materials, slow processes, and the kind of detail that rewards a closer look. These art forms act as a portal to a younger version of myself who loved rocks and crystals, climbing trees, and painting my imagination onto paper.

When I'm not making art, you'll find me playing piano, guitar, or French horn, singing (you might catch me playing a gig at a local coffee shop), digging in my garden, or curled up on the couch with my cat and a cozy video game.

I'm open to consignment and display arrangements with shops, restaurants, and galleries. I would love to collaborate! And for however long you stay, I'm glad you're here.
